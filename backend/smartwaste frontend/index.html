SmartWaste Network

Industrial Waste Exchange Platform

Connecting industries to reduce waste and create a circular economy

💡 The Concept

SmartWaste Network is a virtual marketplace where industries can post waste materials (metal scraps, used oils, packaging materials, etc.) that other industries can reuse.

By connecting waste producers with waste users, we transform industrial waste into valuable resources, reducing landfill waste and saving costs for businesses.

Services We Provide

📦

Post Your Waste

Industries can list available waste materials like metal scraps, used oils, packaging, and more for others to reuse.

🔍

Request Materials

Search and request specific waste materials you need for your manufacturing process.

🗺

Map-Based Discovery

Find available materials nearby using our interactive map interface.

📊

Live Waste Statistics

Track total waste reused and your environmental impact in real-time.

🏆 Reward System

Companies and students earn "EcoTrade Points" for each successful waste exchange transaction.

How It Works:

  • ✅ Earn points for every successful waste transaction
  • 📊 Points displayed on a public leaderboard
  • 🌟 Reach "Net Zero Trader" status after reusing a certain tonnage
  • 🏅 Compete with other companies for top sustainability rankings
