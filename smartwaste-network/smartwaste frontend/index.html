💡 The Concept

SmartWaste Network is a marketplace where industries can post or request waste materials such as metal scrap, used oils, packaging etc.

This creates a circular economy where waste becomes a resource for another industry.

How to navigate:
  1. Click "Navigate" on a listing (or marker). The app will try to get your current location.
  2. If permitted, Google Maps will open with directions from your location to the waste location.
  3. Choose mode (Driving/Walking) and tap Start in Google Maps for step-by-step guidance.
